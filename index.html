Cub Scout Dens

We have dens for boys and girls of all elementary aged scouts from Kindegarten to Fifth Grade.

Cub Scouts is a great way for boys and girls to get their feet wet (litterally sometimes) in the outdoors and get camping experience as well in a safe fun-filled experience with trained adult leaders.

About Pack 21

We meet at Martha Bowman United Methodist Church on Bass Road which is our chartered organizaiton. We meet most Monday nights from 6 PM until 7 PM and combine both indoor activities as well as outdoor when the weather permits. MBUMC provides a nice campus with plenty of green space for us to spread out and enjoy the outdoors.
